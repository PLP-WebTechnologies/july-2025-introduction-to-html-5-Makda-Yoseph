About Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets.

They are known for their agility and playful behavior.

Cats have been associated with humans for at least 9,500 years.

Learn more about cat breeds

Cat Breeds

  • Siamese

    • Known for their striking blue almond-shaped eyes and short coat.

  • Persian

    • Recognized for their long, luxurious fur and flat face.

  • Maine Coon

    • One of the largest domesticated cat breeds, known for their tufted ears and bushy tails.

  • Ragdoll

    • Known for their striking blue eyes and affectionate nature.

  • British Shorthair

    • Recognized for their dense, plush coat and round face.

  • Abyssinian

    • One of the oldest known cat breeds, known for their ticked coat and playful personality.
